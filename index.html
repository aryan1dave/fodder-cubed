The Process

From seed to feed in five days

A continuous cycle that produces fresh, nutrient-rich fodder every day.

01

Seeding

BarleyX seeds are measured, sanitized, and loaded onto growing trays. No soil required — just water and controlled light.

02

Germination

Seeds sprout in controlled humidity and temperature. Automated watering begins on schedules optimized for growth.

03

Growth

Roots develop into a dense mat. Shoots reach 15–20cm under LED grow lights. Nutrient content peaks.

04

Harvest

Fresh fodder is removed as a complete mat — roots, shoots, and seeds together. Ready to feed immediately.
